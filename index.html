What is BioMarco

Precision meets pharmacist expertise

BioMarco is a personalized health optimization service led by a licensed pharmacist with decades of clinical experience. The focus is on what standard appointments rarely have time for: a deep, mechanistic reading of your biomarkers, genetic variants, and metabolic patterns — translated into practical, evidence-based action.


This isn't generic wellness advice. It's targeted analysis of the biological signals your body is already sending — interpreted through both clinical training and lived experience with cardiometabolic risk, genomics, and functional nutrition.

Services

What BioMarco offers

All services are delivered within pharmacist scope of practice and complement, not replace, your existing medical care.

01

Cardiometabolic Biomarker Review

Deep interpretation of your lipid panel beyond total cholesterol — including ApoB, Lp(a), LDL particle number, triglyceride/HDL ratio, and inflammatory markers, with a personalized risk narrative and action plan.

ApoB Lp(a) LDL-P hs-CRP Triglycerides
02

Genomic Variant Analysis

Interpretation of whole-genome or SNP data (Sequencing.com, 23andMe, etc.) with a focus on methylation pathways, nutrient metabolism, cardiovascular risk genes, and how your variants shape optimal supplementation choices.

MTHFR COMT MAO-A VDR APOE
03

Personalized Supplement Protocol

Evidence-based supplement strategies designed around your specific biomarkers and genetic variants — with mechanistic rationale for each choice, dosing guidance, and interaction screening by a practicing pharmacist.

Methylation Omega-3 Vitamin D/K2 Magnesium CoQ10
04

Insulin & Metabolic Assessment

Evaluation of insulin resistance markers (fasting insulin, HOMA-IR, glucose patterns, uric acid) with dietary and lifestyle strategies to improve metabolic flexibility and reduce downstream cardiometabolic risk.

HOMA-IR Fasting Insulin HbA1c Uric Acid
Process

How a consultation works

01

Submit your data

Share your most recent lab results, any available genomic data (whole genome, 23andMe, AncestryDNA raw file), and a brief health history. No data, no problem — we can work with what you have and identify what's worth adding.

02

Pharmacist analysis

Your results are reviewed in depth — not by an algorithm, but by a pharmacist trained to see interactions between metabolic pathways, genetic variants, medications, and nutritional status. This takes time, and that's the point.

03

Personalized report & protocol

You receive a structured written report explaining what your data shows, why it matters, and what to do about it — with specific, prioritized recommendations in plain language. No generic printouts.

04

Follow-up consultation

A video or phone call to walk through findings, answer questions, and refine the protocol based on your goals, existing medications, and practical realities. You leave with a clear plan, not a stack of supplements to figure out yourself.
